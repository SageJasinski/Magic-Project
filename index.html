Where to start?

Hello and welcome to the guid to Magic: the Gathering. Because you are here I am guessing you are either interested or begin dragged into this fantastic card strategy game. Well look no further friend, we are here to help you get started. I know it seems like a lot, and it is, but you only need to know a few basic principles in order to get your toes wet. We recommend first looking at The Cards section of this site in order to best learn how to read the cards you are looking at. Then move to the Your Turn section to find out how to play and what happens on everyone's turns. Lastly there is a Cheat Sheet section that hold a list of useful key words that some cards will have on them such as "Lifelink" or "Deathtouch", the cheat sheet section will tell you what those terms mean. Have fun and enjoy playing this game with all your friends.

In 1991 a company called Wizards of the coast what printing role playing games out of then CEO Peter Adkison's basement when Dr.Richard Garfield, a doctoral candidate in combinatorial mathematics, came to them with the idea to produce a board game called "RoboRally." Peter didn't know if they could make a board game so he asked Dr.Richards to come up with a game that could be carried around and played while waiting in lines. a few weeks later Dr.Richards came back with the idea for a card game called Magic. The game was produced and presented at the origins game fair in 1993 where it was so well received that a years supply of cards was bought which was all they had produced and they had to issue an emergency reprint.
